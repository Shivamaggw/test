There are more than 3,39,000 private K-12 schools in India however there is no formal mechanism to evaluate their performance. This gives a lot of autonomy to private schools to operate in the manner best suited. The fact can’t be overlooked that private schools are a way ahead of government schools in providing better academic results but there are a plethora of parameters which accounts for overall development of the students. Academic results do hold an important position but they alone can't be the basis to define a good school. Sand Prints stresses upon the holistic development of the students which demands for other parameters such as Infrastructure, teaching methodologies, co-curricular activities, teacher’s competence etc. to be taken in view.
