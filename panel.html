Fr. George Paul has extensive experience of 37 years in the education sector. He has been associated with numerous schools as the school principal. Father has travelled extensively and has expert knowledge on educational system followed in different countries. Father has been the President of All Indian National Association of Catholic Schools for two terms. He is also a Governing body member of International Association of Catholic Schools (OIEC), Brussles. Father also serves as a member of National Core Committee to study the New Education Policy of Govt. of India. He has many accolades to his name. He was awarded for the best services rendered in education across India by Shri Kapil Sibbal, Ministry of HRD, Govt of India in 2011.

Ms. Rita Wilson has a rich experience in education sector. She has done BA (Hons) from Miranda House, Delhi University and is an M Phil in English literature. She has been in education field for more than 40 years and was the Chief Executive and Secretary of the council for the ISCE, New Delhi. She has written several English books for the school children which have been published by prestigious publishing houses like Blackie & Sons and S. Chand & Co. Her expertise include curriculum design, public speaking, staff development, teacher training, editing and research.
